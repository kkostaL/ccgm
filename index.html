About CCGM

The Centre for Cardiovascular Genomics and Medicine (CCGM) is a research centre under Lui Che Woo Institute of Innovative Medicine (LCWIIM), and will comprise key faculty members and also the integration of existing faculty members from the Departments and Schools of the Faculty of Medicine. Our mission is to pursue basic and clinical research to foster translational discoveries in pediatric and adult cardiovascular diseases to facilitate disease diagnosis and the development of precision therapeutics. This will entail the use of state of the art systems approach with genomic technologies and big data to elucidate the genetic, epigenetic, and developmental mechanisms of disease pathogenesis. Mechanistic insights gained will be leveraged in cell-based assays and animal model systems for the development of customized therapeutics.
